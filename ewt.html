LP1 - EMERGING WEB TRENDS

This is the beginning of the Fall semester 2018 and in this class we are studying trends that are being used by Website Design/Developers. While there are several "old reliable" designs, more and more designers are going with simple, yet easy to follow layouts. Websites are becoming more user-friendly, and also are designed to guide the user to areas of a page that are pertinent to the message being portrayed. I have listed some of the trends, old and new, that are being used in today's designs.

~Cathy Z.

Use of Gradients

"A gradient creates visual interest and helps move users through a design. The eye will land on one area of color and the change between hues and light and dark areas helps shift focus across the screen."

Polygonal Shapes & Geometric Layers

"One of the most distinct web design trends of 2018 focuses on geometric themes, specifically polygons and layered shapes. Chances are you recognize this style when you see it, but to put a precise definition on it, a polygon is any closed-off shape with straight lines, typically 3-5 sides. This trend includes every floating triangle and square you see, but also original shapes that fit the definition."

My Resource

Bold Lines to Grab Attention

When using thick lines, you want to pay attention to both color and intersection points. Color will determine where the user’s attention goes, whether drawing attention to or away from the lines. Intersection points inherently become focal points, so use them to your advantage.

My Resource

 

Box Shadows

 

Light and shadow can add qualities like depth, dimension, perspective, realism, and visual interest to your designs. This can simultaneously draw viewers into your design and make it seem to pop off the page or screen.

My Resource

Simple Designs

Effective website design is about communicating as much as possible with as few elements as possible. Overly flashy websites tend to drive people away. Instead, keep it simple and don't add unnecessary distractions to your site. I feel this is the best way to bring people to your site and keep them.
